Research Interests

topics & directions
I am particularly interested in logic, algebra, and category theory, especially in the ways these areas interact with one another. I am motivated by understanding how different formal frameworks describe similar mathematical phenomena, and what is gained or lost by changing perspective.
  • In logic, I am especially drawn to model theory, both in its classical form and in categorical approaches via accessible categories.
  • From the algebraic perspective, my interests include semigroup theory, as well as questions of decidability in algebra and rewriting systems.
  • Within category theory, I am most interested in its connections with logic, in particular through topos-theoretic ideas.
